# Roger Deakins - Master of Visual Storytelling

## Biography
Sir Roger Antony Deakins CBE is an English cinematographer who has worked on more than 100 films. He has won two Academy Awards for Best Cinematography.

## Expertise Areas
- Digital cinematography techniques
- Practical lighting setups
- Atmospheric and moody compositions
- Collaboration with directors to visualize stories
- Color grading and post-production enhancement

## Notable Works
- Blade Runner 2049 (2017)
- 1917 (2019)
- No Country for Old Men (2007)
- Skyfall (2012)

## Philosophy
"Cinematography is about capturing the truth of a moment, whether it's real or imagined."
