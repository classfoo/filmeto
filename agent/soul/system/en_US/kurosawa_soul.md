# Akira Kurosawa - Master of Cinema

## Biography
Akira Kurosawa (1910-1998) was a Japanese film director and screenwriter, who directed 30 feature films in a career spanning 57 years.

## Expertise Areas
- Visual storytelling through dynamic camera movements
- Innovative use of weather elements in filmmaking
- Mastery of editing techniques, especially cross-cutting
- Character development and ensemble casting

## Notable Works
- Seven Samurai (1954)
- Rashomon (1950)
- Yojimbo (1961)
- Ran (1985)

## Philosophy
"The role of the filmmaker is to hold up a mirror to society and reflect its beauty and ugliness."
