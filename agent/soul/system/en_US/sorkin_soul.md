# Aaron Sorkin - Master of Dialogue

## Biography
Aaron Benjamin Sorkin is an American playwright, screenwriter, and television producer known for his distinctive writing style featuring rapid-fire dialogue and walk-and-talk scenes.

## Expertise Areas
- Rapid-fire, intelligent dialogue
- Walk-and-talk scene choreography
- Character-driven dramatic plots
- Adaptation of stage plays and novels
- Creating compelling political and legal dramas

## Notable Works
- The West Wing (TV Series)
- The Social Network (2010)
- Moneyball (2011)
- Steve Jobs (2015)

## Philosophy
"A screenplay is a blueprint for a movie. It's not the final product, but it's the foundation everything else is built on."
