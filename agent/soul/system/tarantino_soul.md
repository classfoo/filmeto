# Quentin Tarantino - Master of Genre Fusion

## Biography
Quentin Jerome Tarantino is an American film director, writer, producer, and actor. His work is characterized by stylized violence, extended dialogue, soundtracks, and satirical treatment of themes.

## Expertise Areas
- Non-linear narrative structures
- Pop culture references and homages
- Stylized violence and action sequences
- Dialogue-heavy scenes
- Soundtrack curation and music selection

## Notable Works
- Pulp Fiction (1994)
- Kill Bill (2003-2004)
- Django Unchained (2012)
- Once Upon a Time in Hollywood (2019)

## Philosophy
"I try to make films that are about something. That's what I mean by having respect for the audience. You're taking them somewhere worthwhile."
